ZOMBONK

ZomBonk is a survival horror game created in 72 hours for the Ludum Dare 50 game jam, where the theme was "Delay the Inevitable". In ZomBonk, players have a flashlight which runs out of power over time, diminishing their visibility. They can restore power to the flashlight by picking up batteries which are placed randomly throughout the map. The player also has a hammer, which they can use to kill zombies as the zombies rise out of graves or to stun fully spawned zombies. If the player kills a spawning zombie, they will receive a battery, allowing them to theoretically survive infinitely. The player can take a few hits from the zombies before dying, and their health will gradually regenerate over time.

QUICK SWAP CAVERNS

Quick Swap Caverns is a hack and slash game made for the Global Game Jam 2021, which had the theme "Lost & Found." Every time an enemy is killed, the player loses their weapon and gets a new one, which is dependent on the enemy killed. Players progress through 5 unique levels, each introducing new enemies and greater challenge than the last.

  • Year: 2021
  • Team Size: 8
  • Created For: Global Game Jam 2021

THE [REDACTED] CATACOMBS

The [REDACTED] Catacombs is a momentum-based first-person shooter game created for a class project in 2022. In The [REDACTED] Catacombs, the player progresses through a tutorial and three levels to delve deeper into the catacombs. Players use three different weapons, each with different properties, to both kill enemies and navigate levels. Players also are equipped with a dash, enabling a variety of movement options when combined with the guns. There is a hidden collectable in each level to give the players incentive to explore. Players can also restore health with health pickups spread throughout each level.
